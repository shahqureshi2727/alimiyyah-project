# Tafsir-103 - Surah Al-Asr

## Topic: Surah Al-Asr #tafsir/asr

- Revelation: Makki
- Arabic source: Quran.com Quran Foundation API (https://api.quran.com/api/v4/quran/verses/uthmani and /indopak)

### Ayah 1

- **[TFS-ASR-01]** By the passage of time! · src: Tafsir-103 "Ayah 1" ^TFS-ASR-01
- Uthmani: وَٱلْعَصْرِ
- IndoPak: وَالۡعَصۡرِۙ
- Notes:
  - Asr can mean the time of Asr, time in general, the era or passing ages, or the lifetime of the Prophet ﷺ.

### Ayah 2

- **[TFS-ASR-02]** Surely humanity is in loss · src: Tafsir-103 "Ayah 2" ^TFS-ASR-02
- Uthmani: إِنَّ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ لَفِى خُسْرٍ
- IndoPak: اِنَّ الۡاِنۡسَانَ لَفِىۡ خُسۡرٍۙ
- Notes:
  - The greatest loss includes loss in time, opportunities, and the Hereafter.

### Ayah 3

- **[TFS-ASR-03]** except those who have faith, do good, and urge each other to the truth, and urge each other to perseverance. · src: Tafsir-103 "Ayah 3" ^TFS-ASR-03
- Uthmani: إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ وَتَوَاصَوْا۟ بِٱلْحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوْا۟ بِٱلصَّبْرِ
- IndoPak: اِلَّا الَّذِيۡنَ اٰمَنُوۡا وَ عَمِلُوا الصّٰلِحٰتِ وَتَوَاصَوۡا بِالۡحَقِّ ۙ وَتَوَاصَوۡا بِالصَّبۡرِ
- Notes:
  - The saved group combines iman, righteous deeds, urging each other to truth, and urging each other to perseverance.
