# Tafsir-107 - Surah Al-Ma'un

## Topic: Surah Al-Ma'un #tafsir/mau

- Revelation: Makki
- Arabic source: Quran.com Quran Foundation API (https://api.quran.com/api/v4/quran/verses/uthmani and /indopak)

### Ayah 1

- **[TFS-MAU-01]** Have you seen the one who denies the Judgment? · src: Tafsir-107 "Ayah 1" ^TFS-MAU-01
- Uthmani: أَرَءَيْتَ ٱلَّذِى يُكَذِّبُ بِٱلدِّينِ
- IndoPak: اَرَءَيۡتَ الَّذِىۡ يُكَذِّبُ بِالدِّيۡنِؕ
- Notes:
  - Al-Din can refer to the commands of Allah or the Day of Qiyamah.

### Ayah 2

- **[TFS-MAU-02]** That is the one who repulses the orphan · src: Tafsir-107 "Ayah 2" ^TFS-MAU-02
- Uthmani: فَذَٰلِكَ ٱلَّذِى يَدُعُّ ٱلْيَتِيمَ
- IndoPak: فَذٰلِكَ الَّذِىۡ يَدُعُّ الۡيَتِيۡمَۙ
- Notes:
  - Yadu'u means pushes away or treats harshly.
  - Yatim is a child who lost their father before puberty.

### Ayah 3

- **[TFS-MAU-03]** And does not encourage the feeding of the poor · src: Tafsir-107 "Ayah 3" ^TFS-MAU-03
- Uthmani: وَلَا يَحُضُّ عَلَىٰ طَعَامِ ٱلْمِسْكِينِ
- IndoPak: وَ لَا يَحُضُّ عَلٰى طَعَامِ الۡمِسۡكِيۡنِؕ
- Notes:
  - La yahuddu means does not encourage, urge, or motivate others.
  - The problem is not only action but mindset: if I encourage others, I will also be expected to give.

### Ayah 4

- **[TFS-MAU-04]** So woe to those who pray · src: Tafsir-107 "Ayah 4" ^TFS-MAU-04
- Uthmani: فَوَيْلٌ لِّلْمُصَلِّينَ
- IndoPak: فَوَيۡلٌ لِّلۡمُصَلِّيۡنَۙ
- Notes:
  - Wayl means woe, destruction, and ruin.
  - Wayl can also refer to a valley or place within Hell.
  - Al-musallin means those who pray.

### Ayah 5

- **[TFS-MAU-05]** Those who are unmindful of their prayers · src: Tafsir-107 "Ayah 5" ^TFS-MAU-05
- Uthmani: ٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ عَن صَلَاتِهِمْ سَاهُونَ
- IndoPak: الَّذِيۡنَ هُمۡ عَنۡ صَلَاتِهِمۡ سَاهُوۡنَۙ
- Notes:
  - Being unmindful includes delaying prayer past its time and being negligent or careless about it.

### Ayah 6

- **[TFS-MAU-06]** those who show off · src: Tafsir-107 "Ayah 6" ^TFS-MAU-06
- Uthmani: ٱلَّذِينَ هُمْ يُرَآءُونَ
- IndoPak: الَّذِيۡنَ هُمۡ يُرَآءُوۡنَۙ
- Notes:
  - Showing off includes acting out of embarrassment, social pressure, or for display.
  - Signs of ikhlas include consistency in private and public, dislike of praise, accepting advice, dislike of leadership, and self-awareness.

### Ayah 7

- **[TFS-MAU-07]** And they refuse to show small acts of kindness · src: Tafsir-107 "Ayah 7" ^TFS-MAU-07
- Uthmani: وَيَمْنَعُونَ ٱلْمَاعُونَ
- IndoPak: وَيَمۡنَعُوۡنَ الۡمَاعُوۡنَ
- Notes:
  - Ibn Mas'ud said that in the time of the Prophet ﷺ, al-Ma'un was understood as lending a bucket, cooking pot, or small household tools.
  - Ibn Abbas interpreted it as zakat.

## Flags

- Arabic ayat were not extracted from the Canva PDF text layer; the PDF text stream drops/fragments IndoPak and Madani glyphs, so canonical Quran.com Uthmani and IndoPak text is used for Arabic fields.
