# Tafsir-109 - Surah Al-Kafirun

## Topic: Surah Al-Kafirun #tafsir/kaf

- Revelation: Makki
- Arabic source: Quran.com Quran Foundation API (https://api.quran.com/api/v4/quran/verses/uthmani and /indopak)

### Ayah 1

- **[TFS-KAF-01]** Say, "O you disbelievers!" · src: Tafsir-109 "Ayah 1" ^TFS-KAF-01
- Uthmani: قُلْ يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلْكَـٰفِرُونَ
- IndoPak: قُلۡ يٰۤاَيُّهَا الۡكٰفِرُوۡنَۙ
- Notes:
  - Three major terms related to disbelief are kufr, shirk, and nifaq.

### Ayah 2

- **[TFS-KAF-02]** I do not worship what you worship, · src: Tafsir-109 "Ayah 2" ^TFS-KAF-02
- Uthmani: لَآ أَعْبُدُ مَا تَعْبُدُونَ
- IndoPak: لَاۤ اَعۡبُدُ مَا تَعۡبُدُوۡنَۙ
- Notes:
  - Ibadah is a comprehensive term for everything Allah loves and is pleased with from statements and actions, whether inward or outward.
  - Ibadah entails unconditional obedience, love for Allah above all else, khashyah, sincerity, and accordance with the Sunnah.

### Ayah 3

- **[TFS-KAF-03]** nor do you worship what I worship. · src: Tafsir-109 "Ayah 3" ^TFS-KAF-03
- Uthmani: وَلَآ أَنتُمْ عَـٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعْبُدُ
- IndoPak: وَلَاۤ اَنۡـتُمۡ عٰبِدُوۡنَ مَاۤ اَعۡبُدُ​ ۚ
- Notes:
  - Allah rejects the proposal completely.
  - Even if the disbelievers performed acts directed to Allah, their worship would not be accepted because it was corrupted by shirk.

### Ayah 4

- **[TFS-KAF-04]** I will never worship what you worship, · src: Tafsir-109 "Ayah 4" ^TFS-KAF-04
- Uthmani: وَلَآ أَنَا۠ عَابِدٌ مَّا عَبَدتُّمْ
- IndoPak: وَلَاۤ اَنَا عَابِدٌ مَّا عَبَدۡتُّمۡۙ
- Notes:
  - The repetition emphasizes rejection across present and future, action and acceptance, and action and methodology.

### Ayah 5

- **[TFS-KAF-05]** nor will you ever worship what I worship. · src: Tafsir-109 "Ayah 5" ^TFS-KAF-05
- Uthmani: وَلَآ أَنتُمْ عَـٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعْبُدُ
- IndoPak: وَ لَاۤ اَنۡـتُمۡ عٰبِدُوۡنَ مَاۤ اَعۡبُدُ ؕ
- Notes:
  - The repetition emphasizes rejection across present and future, action and acceptance, and action and methodology.

### Ayah 6

- **[TFS-KAF-06]** You have your way, and I have my Way · src: Tafsir-109 "Ayah 6" ^TFS-KAF-06
- Uthmani: لَكُمْ دِينُكُمْ وَلِىَ دِينِ
- IndoPak: لَـكُمۡ دِيۡنُكُمۡ وَلِىَ دِيۡنِ
- Notes:
  - By placing lakum and li first, the Qur'an conveys exclusivity (hasr).
  - Your religion is only for you and my religion is only for me; there will be no exchange, compromise, or mixing between the two.
  - This is not indifference or permission to believe whatever one wants, but a final declaration.
