# Tafsir-108 - Surah Al-Kawthar

## Topic: Surah Al-Kawthar #tafsir/kaw

- Revelation: Makki
- Arabic source: Quran.com Quran Foundation API (https://api.quran.com/api/v4/quran/verses/uthmani and /indopak)

### Ayah 1

- **[TFS-KAW-01]** Indeed, We have granted you abundant goodness · src: Tafsir-108 "Ayah 1" ^TFS-KAW-01
- Uthmani: إِنَّآ أَعْطَيْنَـٰكَ ٱلْكَوْثَرَ
- IndoPak: اِنَّاۤ اَعۡطَيۡنٰكَ الۡكَوۡثَرَؕ
- Notes:
  - Al-Kawthar refers to the river of Al-Kawthar in Jannah and to abundant goodness.

### Ayah 2

- **[TFS-KAW-02]** So pray and sacrifice to your Lord · src: Tafsir-108 "Ayah 2" ^TFS-KAW-02
- Uthmani: فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَٱنْحَرْ
- IndoPak: فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَانۡحَرۡ ؕ
- Notes:
  - The verse mentions two acts of shukr: salah as worship of the body and nahr as worship through wealth.

### Ayah 3

- **[TFS-KAW-03]** Indeed your enemy is truly cut off · src: Tafsir-108 "Ayah 3" ^TFS-KAW-03
- Uthmani: إِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ ٱلْأَبْتَرُ
- IndoPak: اِنَّ شَانِئَكَ هُوَ الۡاَبۡتَرُ
- Notes:
  - The Quraysh thought the Prophet ﷺ would be forgotten.
  - His enemies are remembered only because of their opposition to him.
