# Tafsir-105 - Surah Al-Fil

## Topic: Surah Al-Fil #tafsir/fil

- Revelation: Makki
- Arabic source: Quran.com Quran Foundation API (https://api.quran.com/api/v4/quran/verses/uthmani and /indopak)

### Ayah 1

- **[TFS-FIL-01]** Have you not seen how your Lord dealt with the Army of the Elephant? · src: Tafsir-105 "Ayah 1" ^TFS-FIL-01
- Uthmani: أَلَمْ تَرَ كَيْفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِأَصْحَـٰبِ ٱلْفِيلِ
- IndoPak: اَلَمۡ تَرَ كَيۡفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِاَصۡحٰبِ الۡفِيۡلِؕ
- Notes:
  - Scholars discussed whether Allah is addressing the People of Makkah or Allah's Messenger ﷺ.

### Ayah 2

- **[TFS-FIL-02]** Did He not make their plan go astray? · src: Tafsir-105 "Ayah 2" ^TFS-FIL-02
- Uthmani: أَلَمْ يَجْعَلْ كَيْدَهُمْ فِى تَضْلِيلٍ
- IndoPak: اَلَمۡ يَجۡعَلۡ كَيۡدَهُمۡ فِىۡ تَضۡلِيۡلٍۙ
- Notes:
  - Their kayd was a carefully prepared attack on the Ka'bah.
  - Allah made it tadlil: completely failed, wasted, and misdirected.

### Ayah 3

- **[TFS-FIL-03]** For He sent against them flocks of birds · src: Tafsir-105 "Ayah 3" ^TFS-FIL-03
- Uthmani: وَأَرْسَلَ عَلَيْهِمْ طَيْرًا أَبَابِيلَ
- IndoPak: وَّاَرۡسَلَ عَلَيۡهِمۡ طَيۡرًا اَبَابِيۡلَۙ
- Notes:
  - The use of 'ala often appears when Allah sends punishment from above, showing His overwhelming power and dominance.
  - The indefinite form tayran suggests great numbers, different kinds of birds, and a sense of awe and terror.

### Ayah 4

- **[TFS-FIL-04]** that pelted them with stones of baked clay · src: Tafsir-105 "Ayah 4" ^TFS-FIL-04
- Uthmani: تَرْمِيهِم بِحِجَارَةٍ مِّن سِجِّيلٍ
- IndoPak: تَرۡمِيۡهِمۡ بِحِجَارَةٍ مِّنۡ سِجِّيۡلٍۙ
- Notes:
  - Rama / yarmi means to throw from a distance or cast something toward a specific target.
  - Each bird carried three stones: two in its claws and one in its beak.
  - The stone would land on a person, hit their head, and come out from the other side.

### Ayah 5

- **[TFS-FIL-05]** leaving them like chewed up straw · src: Tafsir-105 "Ayah 5" ^TFS-FIL-05
- Uthmani: فَجَعَلَهُمْ كَعَصْفٍ مَّأْكُولٍۭ
- IndoPak: فَجَعَلَهُمۡ كَعَصۡفٍ مَّاۡكُوۡلٍ
- Notes:
  - The image shows complete, humiliating destruction through something Arabs would immediately recognize.
