# Fiqh-05 — Faraidh of Wudhu and Misc Rulings

## Topic: Wudhu  #fiqh/wudhu

- **[FQH-WUD-29]** Farḍ 1 of wuḍūʾ is washing the face, defined as the area from the hairline of
  the forehead to the bottom of the chin (length) and ear to ear (width), including the eyelids
  and any pierced areas. · src: Fiqh-05 "Fardh 1 – Washing the Face" ^FQH-WUD-29
- **[FQH-WUD-30]** Partial washing of the face invalidates this farḍ — the entire defined area
  must be washed completely. · src: Fiqh-05 "Fardh 1 – Washing the Face" ^FQH-WUD-30
- **[FQH-WUD-31]** Farḍ 2 of wuḍūʾ is washing the hands and arms up to and including the elbows;
  water must reach the entire forearm from the fingertips to the elbow. · src: Fiqh-05 "Fardh 2 –
  Washing the Arms" ^FQH-WUD-31
- **[FQH-WUD-32]** Farḍ 3 of wuḍūʾ is wiping (masḥ) a quarter of the head, which may be performed
  on the forelock (front), the back of the head, or either of the two sides (temples). · src:
  Fiqh-05 "Fardh 3 – Wiping the Head" ^FQH-WUD-32
- **[FQH-WUD-33]** Farḍ 4 of wuḍūʾ is washing the feet up to and including the ankles, ensuring
  water reaches the entire foot, including between the toes and around the ankles. · src:
  Fiqh-05 "Fardh 4 – Washing the feet" ^FQH-WUD-33
- **[FQH-WUD-34]** For a thick beard, only the outer part must be washed during wuḍūʾ; for a thin
  beard, water should reach the skin beneath it. · src: Fiqh-05 "Misc Rulings — Beard"
  ^FQH-WUD-34
- **[FQH-WUD-35]** Beard hair extending beyond the boundary of the face does not need to be
  washed during wuḍūʾ, as it is not considered part of the face. · src: Fiqh-05 "Misc Rulings —
  Beard" ^FQH-WUD-35
- **[FQH-WUD-36]** The hidden part of the lips (when closed) does not need to be washed in wuḍūʾ;
  the visible part is considered part of the face and must be washed. · src: Fiqh-05 "Misc
  Rulings — Lips" ^FQH-WUD-36
- **[FQH-WUD-37]** The inner eyes do not need to be washed during wuḍūʾ, even during a full
  ghusl. · src: Fiqh-05 "Misc Rulings — Eyes" ^FQH-WUD-37
- **[FQH-WUD-38]** Substances blocking the eyelashes or outer eye (e.g. eyeliner, wax, kohl) must
  be removed and the area washed beneath, for wuḍūʾ to be valid. · src: Fiqh-05 "Misc Rulings —
  Eyes" ^FQH-WUD-38
- **[FQH-WUD-39]** If the fingers are tightly closed, they must be separated during wuḍūʾ so that
  water reaches between them. · src: Fiqh-05 "Misc Rulings — Fingers and Nails" ^FQH-WUD-39
- **[FQH-WUD-40]** Long nails covering the fingertips require water to reach underneath them for
  wuḍūʾ to be valid; dirt under the nails or similar minor obstacles do not prevent wuḍūʾ from
  being valid. · src: Fiqh-05 "Misc Rulings — Fingers and Nails" ^FQH-WUD-40
- **[FQH-WUD-41]** Tight rings must be moved during wuḍūʾ to allow water to reach beneath them. ·
  src: Fiqh-05 "Misc Rulings — Jewelry" ^FQH-WUD-41
- **[FQH-WUD-42]** Earrings must be moved during wuḍūʾ if they prevent water from reaching the
  pierced hole. · src: Fiqh-05 "Misc Rulings — Jewelry" ^FQH-WUD-42
- **[FQH-WUD-43]** Water may be passed over medicine applied to wounds or foot cracks during
  wuḍūʾ, when removing it would be necessary or harmful. · src: Fiqh-05 "Misc Rulings — Wounds
  and Medicines" ^FQH-WUD-43
