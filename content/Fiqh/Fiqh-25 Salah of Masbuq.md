# Fiqh-25 — Salah of Masbuq

## Topic: Masbuq  #fiqh/masbuq

- **[FQH-MSB-01]** A masbūq is a person who joins the congregation after the prayer has already
  begun and misses one or more rakʿahs; the missed rakʿahs are completed individually after the
  imam finishes both salaams. · src: Fiqh-25 "Salah of a Masbuq" ^FQH-MSB-01
- **[FQH-MSB-02]** If the imam is standing (qiyām) when a latecomer joins, the latecomer says
  takbīr, joins him standing, and listens quietly. · src: Fiqh-25 "Joining the Congregation"
  ^FQH-MSB-02
- **[FQH-MSB-03]** If the imam is in rukūʿ when a latecomer joins, the latecomer says takbīr,
  stands briefly, then goes into rukūʿ; catching the rukūʿ before the imam rises from it counts
  as having caught that rakʿah. · src: Fiqh-25 "Joining the Congregation" ^FQH-MSB-03
- **[FQH-MSB-04]** A latecomer must always follow the imam and never move ahead of him. · src:
  Fiqh-25 "Important Rules" ^FQH-MSB-04
- **[FQH-MSB-05]** A latecomer should not stand and wait while the imam is still in sajdah. ·
  src: Fiqh-25 "Important Rules" ^FQH-MSB-05
- **[FQH-MSB-06]** If the imam is sitting in the second or fourth rakʿah when a latecomer arrives,
  the latecomer should say takbīr and join him sitting straight away. · src: Fiqh-25 "Important
  Rules" ^FQH-MSB-06
- **[FQH-MSB-07]** If a latecomer arrives after the imam has already stood up from rukūʿ, that
  rakʿah has been missed. · src: Fiqh-25 "Important Rules" ^FQH-MSB-07
- **[FQH-MSB-08]** When the imam finishes both salaams, the masbūq stands and completes the
  rakʿahs he missed. · src: Fiqh-25 "Important Rules" ^FQH-MSB-08
- **[FQH-MSB-09]** A masbūq who missed 1 rakʿah of a two- or four-rakʿah prayer stands after the
  imam's salām and completes it by reciting Duʿāʾ al-Istiftāḥ, Taʿawwudh, Tasmiyah, Sūrah
  al-Fātiḥah, and another sūrah. · src: Fiqh-25 "Two or Four-Rak'ah Salaah — Missed 1 Rak'ah"
  ^FQH-MSB-09
- **[FQH-MSB-10]** A masbūq who missed 2 rakʿahs of a two- or four-rakʿah prayer completes the
  first by reciting Istiftāḥ, Taʿawwudh, Tasmiyah, al-Fātiḥah, and a sūrah, then the second by
  reciting Tasmiyah, al-Fātiḥah, and a sūrah, then after the sajdah of that second rakʿah sits to
  complete the tashahhud, durūd, duʿāʾ, and salām. · src: Fiqh-25 "Missed 2 Rak'ahs" ^FQH-MSB-10
- **[FQH-MSB-11]** A masbūq who missed 3 rakʿahs of a four-rakʿah prayer completes the first by
  reciting Istiftāḥ, Taʿawwudh, Tasmiyah, al-Fātiḥah, and a sūrah; after its sajdah, sits for the
  first tashahhud only; then completes the second rakʿah reciting Tasmiyah, al-Fātiḥah, and a
  sūrah; then completes the third (final) rakʿah reciting only Tasmiyah and al-Fātiḥah, without
  an additional sūrah. · src: Fiqh-25 "Missed 3 Rak'ahs" ^FQH-MSB-11
- **[FQH-MSB-12]** A masbūq who missed all 4 rakʿahs of a four-rakʿah prayer simply prays a
  normal four-rakʿah prayer from beginning to end after the imam's salaam. · src: Fiqh-25 "Missed
  All 4 Rak'ahs" ^FQH-MSB-12
- **[FQH-MSB-13]** A masbūq who missed 1 rakʿah of Maghrib stands after the imam's salām and
  completes it by reciting Duʿāʾ al-Istiftāḥ, Taʿawwudh, Tasmiyah, Sūrah al-Fātiḥah, and another
  sūrah. · src: Fiqh-25 "Maghrib Salaah — Missed 1 Rak'ah" ^FQH-MSB-13
- **[FQH-MSB-14]** A masbūq who missed 2 rakʿahs of Maghrib completes the first by reciting
  Istiftāḥ, Taʿawwudh, Tasmiyah, al-Fātiḥah, and a sūrah; after its sajdah, sits for the first
  tashahhud only; then completes the second (final) rakʿah reciting Tasmiyah, al-Fātiḥah, and a
  sūrah. · src: Fiqh-25 "Maghrib — Missed 2 Rak'ahs" ^FQH-MSB-14
- **[FQH-MSB-15]** A masbūq who missed all 3 rakʿahs of Maghrib simply prays a normal three-rakʿah
  Maghrib prayer after the imam's salaam. · src: Fiqh-25 "Missed All 3 Rak'ahs (Maghrib)"
  ^FQH-MSB-15
