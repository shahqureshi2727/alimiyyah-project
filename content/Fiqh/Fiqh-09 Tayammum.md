# Fiqh-09 — Tayammum (Dry Ablution)

## Topic: Tayammum  #fiqh/tayammum

- **[FQH-TYM-01]** Tayammum (dry ablution) is wiping the face and arms with something pure from
  the top surface of the earth, performed with an intention, by a man or woman who needs to
  perform wuḍūʾ or ghusl. · src: Fiqh-09 "What is Tayammum & Who can do it?" ^FQH-TYM-01
- **[FQH-TYM-02]** Tayammum is permissible when a person is approximately one sharʿī mile (mīl
  sharʿī, roughly 1.16 miles / 1.86 km) away from water. · src: Fiqh-09 "When Is Tayammum
  Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-02
- **[FQH-TYM-03]** Tayammum is permissible in extreme cold. · src: Fiqh-09 "When Is Tayammum
  Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-03
- **[FQH-TYM-04]** Tayammum is permissible during sickness. · src: Fiqh-09 "When Is Tayammum
  Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-04
- **[FQH-TYM-05]** Tayammum is permissible when wounds cover most of the body. · src: Fiqh-09
  "When Is Tayammum Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-05
- **[FQH-TYM-06]** Tayammum is permissible out of fear of thirst. · src: Fiqh-09 "When Is Tayammum
  Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-06
- **[FQH-TYM-07]** Tayammum is permissible out of fear of an enemy or wild animal near the water. ·
  src: Fiqh-09 "When Is Tayammum Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-07
- **[FQH-TYM-08]** Tayammum is permissible when one lacks the means to draw water from a well. ·
  src: Fiqh-09 "When Is Tayammum Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-08
- **[FQH-TYM-09]** Tayammum is permissible out of fear of missing the Janāzah (funeral) prayer. ·
  src: Fiqh-09 "When Is Tayammum Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-09
- **[FQH-TYM-10]** Tayammum is permissible out of fear of missing the ʿĪd prayer. · src: Fiqh-09
  "When Is Tayammum Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-10
- **[FQH-TYM-11]** Tayammum is not permitted out of fear of missing Jumuʿah or a regular
  obligatory prayer. · src: Fiqh-09 "When Is Tayammum Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-11
- **[FQH-TYM-12]** If a person forgets that water is available to them (e.g. in their car) and
  performs tayammum, their Ṣalāh is valid and does not need to be repeated (qaḍāʾ). · src:
  Fiqh-09 "When Is Tayammum Permissible?" ^FQH-TYM-12
- **[FQH-TYM-13]** Dirt or soil is a permissible material for tayammum. · src: Fiqh-09
  "Permissible materials for Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-13
- **[FQH-TYM-14]** Sand is a permissible material for tayammum. · src: Fiqh-09 "Permissible
  materials for Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-14
- **[FQH-TYM-15]** Stones are a permissible material for tayammum. · src: Fiqh-09 "Permissible
  materials for Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-15
- **[FQH-TYM-16]** Limestone is a permissible material for tayammum. · src: Fiqh-09 "Permissible
  materials for Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-16
- **[FQH-TYM-17]** Antimony is a permissible material for tayammum. · src: Fiqh-09 "Permissible
  materials for Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-17
- **[FQH-TYM-18]** Dust, including dust settled on surfaces, is a permissible material for
  tayammum. · src: Fiqh-09 "Permissible materials for Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-18
- **[FQH-TYM-19]** Farḍ 1 of the method of tayammum is to intend to remove ritual impurity. · src:
  Fiqh-09 "Method of Tayammum (Fardh)" ^FQH-TYM-19
- **[FQH-TYM-20]** Farḍ 2 of the method of tayammum is to strike the earth once and wipe the
  entire face. · src: Fiqh-09 "Method of Tayammum (Fardh)" ^FQH-TYM-20
- **[FQH-TYM-21]** Farḍ 3 of the method of tayammum is to strike the earth a second time and wipe
  the arms up to and including the elbows. · src: Fiqh-09 "Method of Tayammum (Fardh)" ^FQH-TYM-21
- **[FQH-TYM-22]** Saying Bismillāh at the beginning is a sunnah of tayammum. · src: Fiqh-09
  "Method of Tayammum (Sunnah)" ^FQH-TYM-22
- **[FQH-TYM-23]** Following the correct sequence (tartīb) is a sunnah of tayammum. · src: Fiqh-09
  "Method of Tayammum (Sunnah)" ^FQH-TYM-23
- **[FQH-TYM-24]** Having no pause between the actions is a sunnah of tayammum. · src: Fiqh-09
  "Method of Tayammum (Sunnah)" ^FQH-TYM-24
- **[FQH-TYM-25]** Shaking the dust or earth off the hands is a sunnah of tayammum. · src: Fiqh-09
  "Method of Tayammum (Sunnah)" ^FQH-TYM-25
- **[FQH-TYM-26]** Spreading the fingers during the strikes is a sunnah of tayammum. · src:
  Fiqh-09 "Method of Tayammum (Sunnah)" ^FQH-TYM-26
- **[FQH-TYM-27]** Anything that invalidates wuḍūʾ or ghusl also invalidates tayammum. · src:
  Fiqh-09 "Nullifiers of Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-27
- **[FQH-TYM-28]** Tayammum is invalidated by the ability to use water once the excuse that
  allowed it no longer exists — e.g. water becomes available, or fear of an animal, enemy,
  illness, or harm is removed. · src: Fiqh-09 "Nullifiers of Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-28
- **[FQH-TYM-29]** One may perform any number of prayers with a single tayammum, as long as it is
  not broken. · src: Fiqh-09 "Miscellaneous Rulings of Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-29
- **[FQH-TYM-30]** Tayammum and wuḍūʾ cannot be combined — one cannot perform half of one and half
  of the other. · src: Fiqh-09 "Miscellaneous Rulings of Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-30
- **[FQH-TYM-31]** Ṣalāh performed with tayammum does not need to be repeated once water later
  becomes available. · src: Fiqh-09 "Miscellaneous Rulings of Tayammum" ^FQH-TYM-31
- **[FQH-TYM-32]** Tayammum may remain valid for months or years, as long as the excuse permitting
  it continues and it is not otherwise broken. · src: Fiqh-09 "Miscellaneous Rulings of Tayammum"
  ^FQH-TYM-32
