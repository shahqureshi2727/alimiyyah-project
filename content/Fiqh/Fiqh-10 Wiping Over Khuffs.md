# Fiqh-10 — Wiping Over Khuffs (المسح على الخفين)

## Topic: Wiping over Khuffs  #fiqh/khuffs

- **[FQH-KHF-01]** For khuffs (leather socks) to be wiped over instead of washing the feet, they
  must cover both ankles. · src: Fiqh-10 "Conditions of the Khuffs" ^FQH-KHF-01
- **[FQH-KHF-02]** Khuffs made from thick wool or similar material are valid for wiping over,
  provided they meet the other conditions. · src: Fiqh-10 "Conditions of the Khuffs" ^FQH-KHF-02
- **[FQH-KHF-03]** Khuffs must be durable and cover the required area of the foot completely to be
  wiped over validly. · src: Fiqh-10 "Conditions of the Khuffs" ^FQH-KHF-03
- **[FQH-KHF-04]** One must have completed wuḍūʾ and be in a state of purity before putting on the
  khuffs, for wiping over them to later be valid. · src: Fiqh-10 "Conditions of the Khuffs"
  ^FQH-KHF-04
- **[FQH-KHF-05]** A resident may wipe over khuffs for one full day and night (24 hours) from the
  moment wuḍūʾ is nullified. · src: Fiqh-10 "Duration of Wiping" ^FQH-KHF-05
- **[FQH-KHF-06]** A traveller may wipe over khuffs for three full days and nights (72 hours) from
  the moment wuḍūʾ is nullified. · src: Fiqh-10 "Duration of Wiping" ^FQH-KHF-06
- **[FQH-KHF-07]** The minimum area that must be wiped on top of each khuff is a surface area
  equal to three of the smallest fingers. · src: Fiqh-10 "Minimum Area to Wipe" ^FQH-KHF-07
- **[FQH-KHF-08]** Each khuff is considered separately when assessing holes in it. · src: Fiqh-10
  "Holes in the Khuffs" ^FQH-KHF-08
- **[FQH-KHF-09]** Wiping over a khuff becomes invalid if the holes in that khuff equal the
  surface area of three of the smallest toes. · src: Fiqh-10 "Holes in the Khuffs" ^FQH-KHF-09
- **[FQH-KHF-10]** The sunnah method of wiping over khuffs is to begin at the toes, spread the
  fingers apart, and wipe once from the toes toward the shin, wiping the top of the khuff, not
  the bottom. · src: Fiqh-10 "Sunnah Method of Wiping" ^FQH-KHF-10
- **[FQH-KHF-11]** Anything that nullifies wuḍūʾ also invalidates the wiping over khuffs. · src:
  Fiqh-10 "What Invalidates Wiping?" ^FQH-KHF-11
- **[FQH-KHF-12]** Removing even one khuff invalidates the wiping over khuffs. · src: Fiqh-10
  "What Invalidates Wiping?" ^FQH-KHF-12
- **[FQH-KHF-13]** The expiration of the permitted time period invalidates the wiping over khuffs.
  · src: Fiqh-10 "What Invalidates Wiping?" ^FQH-KHF-13
- **[FQH-KHF-14]** Most of the foot exiting the khuff invalidates the wiping over that khuff. ·
  src: Fiqh-10 "What Invalidates Wiping?" ^FQH-KHF-14
