# Fiqh-17 — Wajib (Necessary) Acts of Salah

## Topic: Salah Prayer Times  #fiqh/prayer-times

- **[FQH-SLH-43]** Wājib 1 of Ṣalāh is to say the specific words "Allāhu Akbar" for the takbīr,
  rather than a synonym. · src: Fiqh-17 "Necessary Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-43
- **[FQH-SLH-44]** Wājib 2 of Ṣalāh is to recite al-Fātiḥah along with a sūrah or three verses
  after it, in the first two rakʿahs of an obligatory prayer and in all rakʿahs of a voluntary
  prayer. · src: Fiqh-17 "Necessary Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-44
- **[FQH-SLH-45]** This additional recitation (al-Fātiḥah plus a sūrah or three verses) is wājib
  specifically in the first two rakʿahs of the obligatory prayer, not in the later rakʿahs. · src:
  Fiqh-17 "Necessary Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-45
- **[FQH-SLH-46]** Wājib 3 of Ṣalāh is prostrating with the forehead and nose — specifically most
  of the forehead and the hard part of the nose touching the ground. · src: Fiqh-17 "Necessary
  Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-46
- **[FQH-SLH-47]** Wājib 4 of Ṣalāh is being still for a moment in each pillar (rukn) of the
  prayer. · src: Fiqh-17 "Necessary Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-47
- **[FQH-SLH-48]** Wājib 5 of Ṣalāh is the first sitting (qaʿdah ūlā), in a prayer of three or more
  rakʿahs. · src: Fiqh-17 "Necessary Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-48
- **[FQH-SLH-49]** Wājib 6 of Ṣalāh is reciting the full tashahhud in both the first and final
  sittings. · src: Fiqh-17 "Necessary Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-49
- **[FQH-SLH-50]** Wājib 7 of Ṣalāh is saying the salām to end the prayer. · src: Fiqh-17
  "Necessary Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-50
- **[FQH-SLH-51]** Wājib 8 of Ṣalāh is reciting the qunūt in the witr prayer. · src: Fiqh-17
  "Necessary Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-51
- **[FQH-SLH-52]** Wājib 9 of Ṣalāh is saying six extra takbīrs across both ʿĪd prayers. · src:
  Fiqh-17 "Necessary Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-52
- **[FQH-SLH-53]** Wājib 10 of Ṣalāh is that the imam recites the Qurʾān aloud in the first two
  rakʿahs of the prayers that are recited aloud. · src: Fiqh-17 "Necessary Acts (Wajib)"
  ^FQH-SLH-53
- **[FQH-SLH-54]** Wājib 11 of Ṣalāh is silent recitation in the remaining rakʿahs, for both the
  imam and an individual praying alone. · src: Fiqh-17 "Necessary Acts (Wajib)" ^FQH-SLH-54
