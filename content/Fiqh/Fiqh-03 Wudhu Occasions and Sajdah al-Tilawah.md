# Fiqh-03 — Wudhu Occasions and Sajdah al-Tilawah

## Topic: Wudhu  #fiqh/wudhu

- **[FQH-WUD-01]** Wuḍūʾ is farḍ (obligatory) before performing Ṣalāh. · src: Fiqh-03 "Fardh
  Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-01
- **[FQH-WUD-02]** Wuḍūʾ is farḍ before touching the Muṣḥaf (the physical copy of the Qurʾān). ·
  src: Fiqh-03 "Fardh Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-02
- **[FQH-WUD-03]** Wuḍūʾ is farḍ before performing Ṣalāt al-Janāzah (the funeral prayer). · src:
  Fiqh-03 "Fardh Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-03
- **[FQH-WUD-04]** Wuḍūʾ is farḍ before performing Sajdah al-Tilāwah (the prostration of
  recitation). · src: Fiqh-03 "Fardh Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-04

## Topic: Sajdah al-Tilawah  #fiqh/sajdah-tilawah

- **[FQH-SJD-01]** Sajdah al-Tilāwah (سَجْدَة التِّلَاوَة) is a prostration of recitation
  performed when a verse containing a sajdah is recited or heard from the Qurʾān. · src: Fiqh-03
  "What is Sajdah al-Tilawah?" ^FQH-SJD-01
- **[FQH-SJD-02]** Sajdah al-Tilāwah becomes required when a person recites a sajdah verse
  themselves, or hears it being recited live. · src: Fiqh-03 "When is it required?" ^FQH-SJD-02
- **[FQH-SJD-03]** If the same sajdah verse is recited multiple times within one sitting, only one
  sajdah is required — even if repeated ten or a hundred times in that sitting. · src: Fiqh-03
  "Repeating a Sajdah Verse Multiple Times" ^FQH-SJD-03
- **[FQH-SJD-04]** If the same sajdah verse is recited across different sittings, a new sajdah
  becomes wājib each time — e.g. reciting it in the masjid requires one sajdah, and reciting it
  again later at home requires another. · src: Fiqh-03 "Repeating a Sajdah Verse Multiple Times"
  ^FQH-SJD-04
- **[FQH-SJD-05]** The obligation of Sajdah al-Tilāwah applies equally to the one who recites the
  verse and the one who listens to it live. · src: Fiqh-03 "Listener vs Reciter" ^FQH-SJD-05
- **[FQH-SJD-06]** No Sajdah al-Tilāwah is required when a sajdah verse is heard from a recording
  (e.g. CDs, videos, apps) or a replay of a pre-recorded talk. · src: Fiqh-03 "Contemporary
  Application" ^FQH-SJD-06
- **[FQH-SJD-07]** Sajdah al-Tilāwah is required when a sajdah verse is heard live — e.g. via live
  broadcast, masjid transmitters, or live radio/streaming. · src: Fiqh-03 "Contemporary
  Application" ^FQH-SJD-07

## Topic: Wudhu (continued)  #fiqh/wudhu

- **[FQH-WUD-05]** It is mustaḥabb (recommended) to sleep while in a state of wuḍūʾ. · src:
  Fiqh-03 "Mustahab Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-05
- **[FQH-WUD-06]** It is mustaḥabb to perform wuḍūʾ upon waking from sleep. · src: Fiqh-03
  "Mustahab Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-06
- **[FQH-WUD-07]** It is mustaḥabb to maintain continuous wuḍūʾ throughout the day. · src:
  Fiqh-03 "Mustahab Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-07
- **[FQH-WUD-08]** It is mustaḥabb to renew wuḍūʾ after backbiting, slander, lying, or committing
  any sin. · src: Fiqh-03 "Mustahab Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-08
- **[FQH-WUD-09]** It is mustaḥabb to be in wuḍūʾ at the time of every prayer. · src: Fiqh-03
  "Mustahab Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-09
- **[FQH-WUD-10]** It is mustaḥabb to be in wuḍūʾ when reciting the Qurʾān verbally. · src:
  Fiqh-03 "Mustahab Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-10
- **[FQH-WUD-11]** It is mustaḥabb to be in wuḍūʾ when reading or narrating Ḥadīth. · src:
  Fiqh-03 "Mustahab Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-11
- **[FQH-WUD-12]** It is mustaḥabb to be in wuḍūʾ when studying Islamic knowledge. · src: Fiqh-03
  "Mustahab Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-12
