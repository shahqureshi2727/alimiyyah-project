# Fiqh-06 — Sunan, Adab, and Duas of Wudhu

## Topic: Wudhu  #fiqh/wudhu

- **[FQH-WUD-44]** A sunnah, in the context of wuḍūʾ, is an action the Prophet ﷺ regularly
  practiced, though he occasionally left it. · src: Fiqh-06 "Sunnah & Mustahab in Wudhu"
  ^FQH-WUD-44
- **[FQH-WUD-45]** A mustaḥabb/adab action, in the context of wuḍūʾ, is an action not
  consistently practiced by the Prophet ﷺ, but which is recommended. · src: Fiqh-06 "Sunnah &
  Mustahab in Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-45
- **[FQH-WUD-46]** Making the intention (niyyah) is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "17 Sunnah
  of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-46
- **[FQH-WUD-47]** Using the siwāk (tooth-stick) is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "17 Sunnah
  of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-47
- **[FQH-WUD-48]** Mentioning the name of Allah (tasmiyah) at the start is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. ·
  src: Fiqh-06 "17 Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-48
- **[FQH-WUD-49]** Washing the hands up to the wrists at the start is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src:
  Fiqh-06 "17 Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-49
- **[FQH-WUD-50]** Maintaining tartīb (order) in the actions of wuḍūʾ — beginning with what Allah
  mentioned first — is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "17 Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-50
- **[FQH-WUD-51]** Muwālāt (continuity) in wuḍūʾ — not engaging in an unrelated action between
  its acts — is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "17 Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-51
- **[FQH-WUD-52]** Washing each limb three times is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of
  Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-52
- **[FQH-WUD-53]** Rinsing the entire mouth (maḍmaḍah) three times is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src:
  Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-53
- **[FQH-WUD-54]** Sniffing water into the nose and blowing it out (istinshāq/istinthār) is a
  sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-54
- **[FQH-WUD-55]** Running wet fingers through the beard (takhlīl al-liḥyah) is a sunnah of
  wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-55
- **[FQH-WUD-56]** Running wet fingers through the fingers and toes (takhlīl al-aṣābiʿ) is a
  sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-56
- **[FQH-WUD-57]** Wiping the entire head once, and the ears with the same water, is a sunnah of
  wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-57
- **[FQH-WUD-58]** Rubbing the limbs while washing them (dalk) is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src:
  Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-58
- **[FQH-WUD-59]** Starting with the right limb before the left is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src:
  Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-59
- **[FQH-WUD-60]** Starting with the fingers and toes, when washing hands and feet, is a sunnah of
  wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-60
- **[FQH-WUD-61]** Starting from the front of the head when performing masḥ is a sunnah of
  wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-61
- **[FQH-WUD-62]** Wiping the back of the neck is a sunnah of wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-06 "Sunnah of
  Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-62
- **[FQH-WUD-63]** Facing the qiblah while performing wuḍūʾ is a mustaḥabb adab. · src: Fiqh-06
  "Adab/Mustahab" ^FQH-WUD-63
- **[FQH-WUD-64]** Avoiding contact with the water that has already dripped off during one's own
  wuḍūʾ (not reusing splashback) is a mustaḥabb adab. · src: Fiqh-06 "Adab/Mustahab" ^FQH-WUD-64
- **[FQH-WUD-65]** Mentioning Allah's name while washing each limb (not only at the start) is a
  mustaḥabb adab. · src: Fiqh-06 "Adab/Mustahab" ^FQH-WUD-65
- **[FQH-WUD-66]** Not seeking assistance from others — performing wuḍūʾ oneself without help — is
  a mustaḥabb adab. · src: Fiqh-06 "Adab/Mustahab" ^FQH-WUD-66
- **[FQH-WUD-67]** Performing wuḍūʾ before the time of prayer begins, for one who is not excused,
  is a mustaḥabb adab. · src: Fiqh-06 "Adab/Mustahab" ^FQH-WUD-67
- **[FQH-WUD-68]** Reciting the Shahādatayn (the two testimonies of faith) after wuḍūʾ is a
  mustaḥabb adab. · src: Fiqh-06 "Adab/Mustahab" ^FQH-WUD-68
- **[FQH-WUD-69]** Standing and drinking from the leftover water of one's wuḍūʾ is a mustaḥabb
  adab. · src: Fiqh-06 "Adab/Mustahab" ^FQH-WUD-69
- **[FQH-WUD-70]** Making duʿāʾ with the narrated prayers during wuḍūʾ is a mustaḥabb adab. · src:
  Fiqh-06 "Adab/Mustahab" ^FQH-WUD-70
- **[FQH-WUD-71]** While rinsing the mouth during wuḍūʾ, it is mustaḥabb to recite: "اَللّٰهُمَّ
  أَعِنِّي عَلَى تِلَاوَةِ الْقُرْآنِ وَذِكْرِكَ وَشُكْرِكَ وَحُسْنِ عِبَادَتِكَ" — "O Allah, help
  me in the recitation of the Qurʾān, in remembering You, in thanking You, and in perfecting Your
  worship." · src: Fiqh-06 "Dua during Wudhu — While rinsing the mouth" ^FQH-WUD-71
- **[FQH-WUD-72]** While rinsing the nose during wuḍūʾ, it is mustaḥabb to recite: "بِسْمِ اللهِ،
  اَللّٰهُمَّ أَرِحْنِي رَائِحَةَ الْجَنَّةِ وَلَا تُرِحْنِي رَائِحَةَ النَّارِ" — "In the Name of
  Allah. O Allah, let me smell the fragrance of Paradise, and do not let me smell the stench of
  the Hellfire." · src: Fiqh-06 "Dua during Wudhu — While Rinsing the Nose" ^FQH-WUD-72
- **[FQH-WUD-73]** While washing the face during wuḍūʾ, it is mustaḥabb to recite: "بِسْمِ اللهِ،
  اَللّٰهُمَّ بَيِّضْ وَجْهِي يَوْمَ تَبْيَضُّ وُجُوهٌ وَتَسْوَدُّ وُجُوهٌ" — "In the Name of Allah.
  O Allah, illuminate my face on the Day when some faces will be illuminated and others will be
  darkened." · src: Fiqh-06 "Dua during Wudhu — While Washing the Face" ^FQH-WUD-73
- **[FQH-WUD-74]** While washing the right arm during wuḍūʾ, it is mustaḥabb to recite: "بِسْمِ
  اللهِ، اَللّٰهُمَّ أَعْطِنِي كِتَابِي بِيَمِينِي وَحَاسِبْنِي حِسَابًا يَسِيرًا" — "O Allah, give
  me my record in my right hand and make my reckoning easy." · src: Fiqh-06 "Dua during Wudhu —
  While Washing the Arms, Right Arm" ^FQH-WUD-74
- **[FQH-WUD-75]** While washing the left arm during wuḍūʾ, it is mustaḥabb to recite: "بِسْمِ
  اللهِ، اَللّٰهُمَّ لَا تُعْطِنِي كِتَابِي بِشِمَالِي وَلَا مِنْ وَرَاءِ ظَهْرِي" — "O Allah, do
  not give me my record in my left hand, nor from behind my back." · src: Fiqh-06 "Dua during
  Wudhu — Left Arm" ^FQH-WUD-75
- **[FQH-WUD-76]** While wiping the head during wuḍūʾ, it is mustaḥabb to recite: "بِسْمِ اللهِ،
  اَللّٰهُمَّ أَظِلَّنِي تَحْتَ ظِلِّ عَرْشِكَ يَوْمَ لَا ظِلَّ إِلَّا ظِلُّ عَرْشِكَ" — "O Allah,
  shade me under the shade of Your Throne on the Day when there is no shade except Your shade." ·
  src: Fiqh-06 "Dua during Wudhu — While Wiping the Head" ^FQH-WUD-76
- **[FQH-WUD-77]** While wiping the ears during wuḍūʾ, it is mustaḥabb to recite: "بِسْمِ اللهِ،
  اَللّٰهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِي مِنَ الَّذِينَ يَسْتَمِعُونَ الْقَوْلَ فَيَتَّبِعُونَ أَحْسَنَهُ" — "O
  Allah, make me among those who listen to speech and follow the best of it." · src: Fiqh-06 "Dua
  during Wudhu — Wiping the Ears" ^FQH-WUD-77
- **[FQH-WUD-78]** While wiping the neck during wuḍūʾ, it is mustaḥabb to recite: "بِسْمِ اللهِ،
  اَللّٰهُمَّ أَعْتِقْ رَقَبَتِي مِنَ النَّارِ" — "In the Name of Allah. O Allah, free my neck from
  the Fire." · src: Fiqh-06 "Dua during Wudhu — Wiping the Neck" ^FQH-WUD-78
- **[FQH-WUD-79]** While washing the right foot during wuḍūʾ, it is mustaḥabb to recite: "بِسْمِ
  اللهِ، اَللّٰهُمَّ ثَبِّتْ قَدَمَيَّ عَلَى الصِّرَاطِ يَوْمَ تَزِلُّ الْأَقْدَامُ" — "O Allah, make
  my foot firm upon the Ṣirāṭ on the Day when feet will slip." · src: Fiqh-06 "Dua during Wudhu —
  While Washing the Feet, Right Foot" ^FQH-WUD-79
- **[FQH-WUD-80]** While washing the left foot during wuḍūʾ, it is mustaḥabb to recite: "بِسْمِ
  اللهِ، اَللّٰهُمَّ اجْعَلْ ذَنْبِي مَغْفُورًا وَسَعْيِي مَشْكُورًا وَتِجَارَتِي لَنْ تَبُورَ" — "O
  Allah, forgive my sins, accept my efforts, and make my trade never fail." · src: Fiqh-06 "Dua
  during Wudhu — Left Foot" ^FQH-WUD-80

## Flags

- **⚑harakāt FQH-WUD-71 through FQH-WUD-80** — the Arabic text on these slides extracted from the
  source PDF with garbled right-to-left character ordering (a PDF text-layer issue, not a content
  issue), so the Arabic given here was reconstructed from memory of these well-known wuḍūʾ duas
  rather than read directly off the slide. Please check each wording and ḥarakah against the
  actual slide images before moving this deck past `extracted`.
- **FQH-WUD-64** — "Avoiding the water used in Wudhu" was ambiguous phrasing on the source slide
  (no further elaboration given). Rendered here as avoiding reuse of one's own splashback/used
  water; please confirm this is the intended meaning.
- **FQH-WUD-13** — the source slide labels the Ṭawāf occasion "Wajib" while the other four
  occasions on the same list (Salah, Mushaf, Janazah, Sajdah Tilawah) are labelled "Fardh" — this
  looks like a deliberate Ḥanafī Fardh/Wajib distinction rather than an inconsistency, but please
  confirm.
