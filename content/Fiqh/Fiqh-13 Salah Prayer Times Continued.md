# Fiqh-13 — Salah Prayer Times (Continued) and Mustahab Times

## Topic: Salah Prayer Times  #fiqh/prayer-times

- **[FQH-SLH-03]** ʿAṣr prayer time begins when Ẓuhr time ends and lasts until just before
  sunset. · src: Fiqh-13 "Asr" ^FQH-SLH-03
- **[FQH-SLH-04]** Maghrib prayer time begins at the point of sunset and ends with the
  disappearance of the red twilight. · src: Fiqh-13 "Maghrib" ^FQH-SLH-04
- **[FQH-SLH-05]** ʿIshāʾ prayer time begins after Maghrib ends, when the red twilight
  disappears, and lasts until just before Fajr. · src: Fiqh-13 "Isha" ^FQH-SLH-05
- **[FQH-SLH-06]** It is mustaḥabb to pray Fajr when the light has begun to spread (isfār), rather
  than at the very earliest part of its time. · src: Fiqh-13 "Mustahab (Preferred) Prayer Times —
  Fajr" ^FQH-SLH-06
- **[FQH-SLH-07]** It is mustaḥabb to pray Ẓuhr as soon as its time enters in winter, but to delay
  it until it becomes cooler in summer. · src: Fiqh-13 "Mustahab (Preferred) Prayer Times — Zuhr"
  ^FQH-SLH-07
- **[FQH-SLH-08]** It is mustaḥabb to delay ʿAṣr slightly, but before the sun changes colour. ·
  src: Fiqh-13 "Mustahab (Preferred) Prayer Times — Asr & Maghrib" ^FQH-SLH-08
- **[FQH-SLH-09]** It is mustaḥabb to always pray Maghrib immediately when its time begins. · src:
  Fiqh-13 "Mustahab (Preferred) Prayer Times — Asr & Maghrib" ^FQH-SLH-09
- **[FQH-SLH-10]** It is mustaḥabb to delay ʿIshāʾ and pray it before one-third of the night has
  passed — found by dividing the time from sunset to Fajr into three equal parts (e.g. a 12-hour
  night divides into thirds of 4 hours each). · src: Fiqh-13 "Mustahab (Preferred) Prayer Times —
  Isha" ^FQH-SLH-10
- **[FQH-SLH-11]** In cloudy weather, it is recommended to pray ʿAṣr and ʿIshāʾ as soon as their
  time enters, while delaying the other prayers slightly out of caution. · src: Fiqh-13 "Cloudy
  Weather" ^FQH-SLH-11
