# Fiqh-04 — Wudhu Rulings and Conditions

## Topic: Wudhu  #fiqh/wudhu

- **[FQH-WUD-13]** `[madhhab-specific]` Wuḍūʾ is wājib (not farḍ) before performing Ṭawāf (the
  circumambulation of the Kaʿbah) — a Ḥanafī distinction between the two obligation strengths. ·
  src: Fiqh-04 "Fardh Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-13
- **[FQH-WUD-14]** Renewing wuḍūʾ unnecessarily while already in a state of wuḍūʾ is makrūh
  (disliked, not sinful) if done before changing one's place within the same sitting/gathering. ·
  src: Fiqh-04 "Makruh Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-14
- **[FQH-WUD-15]** Renewing wuḍūʾ unnecessarily while already in a state of wuḍūʾ is makrūh if
  done before using that existing wuḍūʾ for an act of worship that requires it. · src: Fiqh-04
  "Makruh Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-15
- **[FQH-WUD-16]** Performing wuḍūʾ using stolen or usurped water — water taken without the
  owner's permission — is ḥarām. · src: Fiqh-04 "Haraam" ^FQH-WUD-16
- **[FQH-WUD-17]** Performing wuḍūʾ using an orphan's water without permission — using their
  property unjustly — is ḥarām. · src: Fiqh-04 "Haraam" ^FQH-WUD-17
- **[FQH-WUD-18]** The conditions of obligation (shurūṭ al-wujūb, شُرُوط الْوُجُوب) for wuḍūʾ are
  the conditions that must be present for wuḍūʾ to become required of a person; if absent, the
  person is not yet responsible for performing wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-04 "Prerequisites for Wudhu
  Obligation" ^FQH-WUD-18
- **[FQH-WUD-19]** ʿAql (عَقْل, sound mind) is a condition of obligation for wuḍūʾ, forming the
  basis of legal responsibility. · src: Fiqh-04 "Prerequisites for Wudhu Obligation" ^FQH-WUD-19
- **[FQH-WUD-20]** Bulūgh (بُلُوغ, reaching puberty/maturity) is a condition of obligation for
  wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-04 "Prerequisites for Wudhu Obligation" ^FQH-WUD-20
- **[FQH-WUD-21]** `[madhhab-specific]` Islam is a condition of obligation for wuḍūʾ; in the
  Ḥanafī view, wuḍūʾ is not required of a non-Muslim. · src: Fiqh-04 "Prerequisites for Wudhu
  Obligation" ^FQH-WUD-21
- **[FQH-WUD-22]** Access to sufficient pure water to wash all the required limbs once is a
  condition of obligation for wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-04 "Prerequisites for Wudhu Obligation"
  ^FQH-WUD-22
- **[FQH-WUD-23]** The termination of menstruation (ḥayḍ), postnatal bleeding (nifās), or a state
  of ritual impurity is a condition of obligation for wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-04 "Prerequisites for
  Wudhu Obligation" ^FQH-WUD-23
- **[FQH-WUD-24]** When the time for prayer is nearly ending, wuḍūʾ becomes obligatory. · src:
  Fiqh-04 "Prerequisites for Wudhu Obligation" ^FQH-WUD-24
- **[FQH-WUD-25]** The conditions of validity (shurūṭ al-ṣiḥḥah, شُرُوط الصِّحَّة) for wuḍūʾ
  determine whether a wuḍūʾ already performed counts as valid; if any is broken, the wuḍūʾ is
  invalid even if attempted. · src: Fiqh-04 "Conditions of validity" ^FQH-WUD-25
- **[FQH-WUD-26]** Complete washing of the skin — water reaching every required area — is a
  condition of validity for wuḍūʾ; even a tiny dry spot invalidates it. · src: Fiqh-04
  "Conditions of validity" ^FQH-WUD-26
- **[FQH-WUD-27]** The ḥadath (the state that breaks wuḍūʾ, e.g. blood, urine, ḥayḍ, nifās) must
  have fully ended before starting wuḍūʾ, as a condition of its validity. · src: Fiqh-04
  "Conditions of validity" ^FQH-WUD-27
- **[FQH-WUD-28]** Removal of any barrier preventing water from reaching the skin — e.g. wax,
  glue, thick cream, nail products, adhesives — is a condition of validity for wuḍūʾ. · src:
  Fiqh-04 "Conditions of validity" ^FQH-WUD-28
