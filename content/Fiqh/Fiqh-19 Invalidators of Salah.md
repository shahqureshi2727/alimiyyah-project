# Fiqh-19 — Invalidators of Salah

## Topic: Salah Prayer Times  #fiqh/prayer-times

- **[FQH-SLH-71]** Human speech — uttering any word outside the prescribed words of prayer, even
  out of forgetfulness — invalidates Ṣalāh. · src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 1. Human Speech"
  ^FQH-SLH-71
- **[FQH-SLH-72]** Excessive movement invalidates Ṣalāh. · src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 2.
  Excessive Movement" ^FQH-SLH-72
- **[FQH-SLH-73]** Eating anything during the prayer, even a small amount, invalidates Ṣalāh. ·
  src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 3. Eating" ^FQH-SLH-73
- **[FQH-SLH-74]** Drinking water or any other liquid during the prayer invalidates Ṣalāh. · src:
  Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 4. Drinking" ^FQH-SLH-74
- **[FQH-SLH-75]** Clearing the throat in a way that produces letters, without a valid excuse,
  invalidates Ṣalāh. · src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 5. Clearing the Throat" ^FQH-SLH-75
- **[FQH-SLH-76]** Crying out loud or moaning due to controlled pain or a calamity invalidates
  Ṣalāh, except when it is due to remembrance of Paradise or Hellfire, which is excused. · src:
  Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 6. Crying or Moaning" ^FQH-SLH-76
- **[FQH-SLH-77]** Greeting someone invalidates Ṣalāh. · src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 7. Greeting
  Someone" ^FQH-SLH-77
- **[FQH-SLH-78]** Shaking hands invalidates Ṣalāh. · src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 8. Shaking
  Hands" ^FQH-SLH-78
- **[FQH-SLH-79]** Responding to someone's speech — even with a phrase from the Qurʾān or dhikr —
  invalidates Ṣalāh. · src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 9. Responding to Speech" ^FQH-SLH-79
- **[FQH-SLH-80]** Turning the torso away from the qiblah by more than 45 degrees on either side
  invalidates Ṣalāh. · src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 10. Turning Away from Qiblah" ^FQH-SLH-80
- **[FQH-SLH-81]** Making duʿāʾ using normal human speech, or asking for something that could be
  asked of a human being, invalidates Ṣalāh. · src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 11. Worldly
  Supplication" ^FQH-SLH-81
- **[FQH-SLH-82]** Extending the hamzah in the takbīr ("Allāhu Akbar") invalidates Ṣalāh. · src:
  Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 12. Extending the Hamzah in takbir" ^FQH-SLH-82
- **[FQH-SLH-83]** Any part of the ʿawrah — at least one-fourth of a limb — becoming uncovered for
  long enough to perform an integral of the Ṣalāh invalidates it. · src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators —
  13. Uncovered Awrah" ^FQH-SLH-83
- **[FQH-SLH-84]** Carrying an unexcused amount of najāsah during the prayer invalidates Ṣalāh. ·
  src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 14. Impurity on Body/Clothing" ^FQH-SLH-84
- **[FQH-SLH-85]** A follower preceding the imam in an integral, or missing an integral the imam
  performed, invalidates that follower's Ṣalāh. · src: Fiqh-19 "Invalidators — 15. Following the
  Imam Incorrectly" ^FQH-SLH-85
