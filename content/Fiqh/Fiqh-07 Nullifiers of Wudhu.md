# Fiqh-07 — Nullifiers of Wudhu

## Topic: Wudhu  #fiqh/wudhu

- **[FQH-WUD-81]** Anything exiting from the two openings (the front or rear private parts) — such
  as urine, stool, wind, madhy, or blood — nullifies wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-07 "Exiting of impurity"
  ^FQH-WUD-81
- **[FQH-WUD-82]** Blood or pus flowing from elsewhere on the body (not the two openings)
  nullifies wuḍūʾ if it flows beyond its source. · src: Fiqh-07 "Exiting of impurity" ^FQH-WUD-82
- **[FQH-WUD-83]** Vomiting a mouthful or more nullifies wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-07 "Exiting of
  impurity" ^FQH-WUD-83
- **[FQH-WUD-84]** If blood comes out from the mouth and predominates over, or is equal to, the
  saliva, wuḍūʾ is nullified. · src: Fiqh-07 "Exiting of impurity" ^FQH-WUD-84
- **[FQH-WUD-85]** Sleeping in a position where the buttocks are not firmly planted on the ground
  nullifies wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-07 "Loss of Awareness" ^FQH-WUD-85
- **[FQH-WUD-86]** Fainting nullifies wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-07 "Loss of Awareness" ^FQH-WUD-86
- **[FQH-WUD-87]** Insanity nullifies wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-07 "Loss of Awareness" ^FQH-WUD-87
- **[FQH-WUD-88]** Intoxication or drunkenness nullifies wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-07 "Loss of Awareness"
  ^FQH-WUD-88
- **[FQH-WUD-89]** Loud laughter during Ṣalāh, by an adult, nullifies wuḍūʾ — a unique nullifier
  specific to laughing while in prayer. · src: Fiqh-07 "A Unique Nullifier" ^FQH-WUD-89
- **[FQH-WUD-90]** Blood that appears on the skin without flowing does not nullify wuḍūʾ. · src:
  Fiqh-07 "Things That Do Not Nullify Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-90
- **[FQH-WUD-91]** Flesh falling off the body without blood flowing does not nullify wuḍūʾ. · src:
  Fiqh-07 "Things That Do Not Nullify Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-91
- **[FQH-WUD-92]** Vomiting less than a mouthful does not nullify wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-07 "Things
  That Do Not Nullify Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-92
- **[FQH-WUD-93]** Vomiting phlegm, whether little or much, does not nullify wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-07
  "Things That Do Not Nullify Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-93
- **[FQH-WUD-94]** Sleeping during prayer while maintaining the correct (sunnah) prayer posture
  does not nullify wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-07 "Things That Do Not Nullify Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-94
- **[FQH-WUD-95]** Sleeping while sitting firmly on the ground does not nullify wuḍūʾ. · src:
  Fiqh-07 "Things That Do Not Nullify Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-95
- **[FQH-WUD-96]** `[madhhab-specific]` Touching one's own private part does not nullify wuḍūʾ. ·
  src: Fiqh-07 "Things That Do Not Nullify Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-96
- **[FQH-WUD-97]** `[madhhab-specific]` Touching a woman does not nullify wuḍūʾ. · src: Fiqh-07
  "Things That Do Not Nullify Wudhu" ^FQH-WUD-97

## Flags

- **FQH-WUD-96, FQH-WUD-97** — the slide states these flatly without naming a madhhab, but both
  are classic points of difference between the Ḥanafī view (given here) and other schools (e.g.
  the Shāfiʿī view that touching a woman without a barrier nullifies wuḍūʾ); tagged
  `[madhhab-specific]` on that basis — please confirm the Ḥanafī attribution.
