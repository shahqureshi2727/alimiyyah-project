## 2. Discussion point

- source_id: HDT-11-S02
- category: note

Have we lost the essence of Ramadan?
The hunger we experience is meant to aid us in abstaining
from sin, not just our diet.
We don’t anticipate the worship, rather we start preparing
for iftaar dawats, trying new recipes, and taraweeh
becomes a time where socializing is the primary objective.
Suhoor Fests and the marketization of Ramadan

^HDT-11-S02

## 4. The purpose of hunger and Fasting

- source_id: HDT-11-S04
- category: note

Fasting is a prescription, prescribed to us by our creator
Hunger builds self-restraint and disciplne
Hunger tames the nafs
Fasting teaches patience and delayed gratification
Fasting is a shield from sin and evil forces
The reward for fasting is unquantifiable

^HDT-11-S04

## 5. Make this Ramadan unforgettable

- source_id: HDT-11-S05
- category: note

Ramadan is a building block
Make a realistic schedule that you can follow
Start preparing from now
Attend Taraweeh every night and praying salah in congregation
Recite a portion of Quran daily (aim for a khatam)
Daily adhkaar
Aim to finish a book of Seerah during this month

^HDT-11-S05
