## 4. Treating animals with ihsaan

- source_id: HDT-21-S04
- category: note

A person must be conscious of Allah in all aspects of life.
Allah SWT has made animals subservient to human beings.
That does not give us the license to be abusive.
The prophet SAW rendered many warnings against those
who show cruelty against animals. Even in acts such as
slaughter, we are to show up most care and efficiency.

^HDT-21-S04
