What happens when a person makes dua?

Not every dua is accepted
There are 3 outcomes when a person makes dua
The dua must be made sincerely
Your dua should not involve sin
Do not be hasty when making dua
          Method of making Dua
Cup your hands
Place them near your chest
Begin your dua with Salawaat
Begin your dua with praises of Allah
Before you make dua for yourself, make dua for your
parents and others
End your dua by sending salutations on rasulullah saw
Wipe over your face
