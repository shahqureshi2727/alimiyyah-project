## 6. Imam Ghazalis explanation of Niyyah

- source_id: HDT-03-S06
- category: explanation

There is no Niyyah in acts of disobedience
Intention is needed in worship and righteous deeds
You can make multiple intentions for a single deed
A strong Niyyah can turn mubah actions into reward

^HDT-03-S06
