## 4. Being gentle in every aspect of life

- source_id: HDT-16-S04
- category: note

Gentleness is a trait that needs to be consciously applied
This quality is not only restricted to human beings but
should be applied in everything
The one who is deprived of rifq is deprived of all good

^HDT-16-S04

## 5. Narrator of this hadith

- source_id: HDT-16-S05
- category: narrator

Abu Hurairah RA

^HDT-16-S05

## 6. Hadith 15

- source_id: HDT-16-S06
- category: hadith_text

‫َا لَّس ِع ُد َم ُو ِع َظ ِبَغ ِر ِه‬
‫ْي‬ ‫ْي ْن‬
“The fortunate one is the one who
takes advice from others.”

^HDT-16-S06

## 7. Learning from the experiences of others

- source_id: HDT-16-S07
- category: note

We can learn from the experiences/life lessons of others
We may not be able to bear the same trials others go
through
If a persons heeds the advice he/she is given, they may be
able to avoid an outcome they may not have expected

^HDT-16-S07
