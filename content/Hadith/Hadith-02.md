## 7. Grave warning on misquoting or lying about the

- source_id: HDT-02-S07
- category: source_note

Hadith of the Prophet (SAW)
The Prophet (SAW) said, “Whoever tells a lie about me intentionally, then let
him take his seat in the fire.” (Al Tirmidhi)
The Prophet (SAW) said, “Ascribing false things to me is not like
ascribing false things to anyone else. Whosoever tells a lie against me
intentionally then surely let him occupy his seat in Hellfire.”
(Al Bukhari)

^HDT-02-S07
