# Hadith-06 - Hadith 6

- category: hadith_text

^HDT-06-S01

## 2. Preview of the Hadith

- source_id: HDT-06-S02
- category: preview

Abu Hurairah RA is the narrator of this hadith
Aqra’ Bin Haabis RA was a leader amongst the arabs in
Jaahiliyyah (period of ignorance). He came with Banu
Tamim to accept Islaam.

^HDT-06-S02

## 3. Abu Hurairah RA

- source_id: HDT-06-S03
- category: source_note

th
Accepted Islaam after the 7 year of Hijri
Narrated the most Hadith
Lived amongst the Ashaabus Suffa
Passed away in Madina 69 - 70 years after Hijri

^HDT-06-S03

## 4. Core aspects and context

- source_id: HDT-06-S04
- category: context

It was unusual for the Arabs to express love as it was
deemed as a sign of softness
The prophet (saw) is teaching the Ummah that
expressing ones love is indeed a sign that Allah is
showing mercy to that individual

^HDT-06-S04

## 5. Hadith 4

‫َيْر َح ْم ُيْر َح ْم‬ ‫اَّل‬ ‫َم‬

^HDT-06-S05

## 6. The meaning of Rahma

- source_id: HDT-06-S06
- category: explanation

To have softness and a tender feeling initially
The feeling then translates to action through
compassion and benevelonce

^HDT-06-S06
