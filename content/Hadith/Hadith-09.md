## 2. Jabir ibn Abdillah Al Ansari

- source_id: HDT-09-S02
- category: note

He was the youngest companion present during the
second Bay’atul ‘Aqabah
He was considered a Mufti amongst the companions
From the group of companions who were present during
the second Bay’ah, he was the last to pass away.
He passed away approximately at the age of 94.

^HDT-09-S02

## 3. Preview of the Hadith

- source_id: HDT-09-S03
- category: preview

The messenger SAW is giving us a description of what the
consequence of oppression will be on the Day of
Judgment

^HDT-09-S03

## 6. Different forms of Dhulm (oppression)

- source_id: HDT-09-S06
- category: note

The worst form of oppression is shirk (associating partners
with Allah)
Oppressing yourself (persisting in sin, neglecting
obligations)
Oppressing others (physical, social, emotional, financial)

^HDT-09-S06
