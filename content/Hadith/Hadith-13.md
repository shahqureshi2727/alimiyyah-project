## 4. What is Tawbah?

- source_id: HDT-13-S04
- category: note

Tawbah is to repent. Specifically repentance from major
sins.
Minor deeds can be forgiven via many deeds. Tawbah is
necessary to have your major sins forgiven.

^HDT-13-S04

## 5. The conditions for a successful Tawbah

- source_id: HDT-13-S05
- category: note

Being sincere to Allah with your tawbah
To recognize that you have committed a grave sin
You must have a feeling of regret over the action committed
You must have a strong resolve that you will never return to this
action again

^HDT-13-S05
